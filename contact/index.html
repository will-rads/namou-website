FAQs

Questions we often hear

Can foreigners buy freehold land in Ras Al Khaimah?

Yes. Foreign buyers can own property in designated freehold areas and projects in Ras Al Khaimah. Status is project-specific, so each plot should be confirmed before any offer.

Can I get a Golden Visa by investing in RAK real estate?

A real estate investor may qualify for a renewable UAE Golden Visa when the property or group of properties meets the AED 2 million minimum and the relevant authority requirements.

What taxes apply to rental income and capital gains in RAK?

The UAE does not levy personal income tax, so individual rental income and capital gains are generally outside personal income tax. Business activity can still be subject to UAE corporate tax rules.
