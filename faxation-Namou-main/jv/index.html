Common JV questions.

What's the difference between buying land and a joint venture?

A direct purchase gives you full ownership and control — you pay upfront and own the asset outright. A joint venture pools your capital with Namou's vetted partners to share in development returns without managing construction yourself. JVs allow smaller entry points and expert execution.

What ROI can I realistically expect from a JV?

Returns vary by structure. Build-to-sell JVs typically target 20–30% margins over 24–36 months. Build-to-lease structures yield 7–10% operating returns plus exit appreciation. Hospitality JVs combine operator revenues with long-term asset appreciation. All projections are modelled live on your video call.

Reference: Knight Frank — UAE Development Returns

How is the Wynn Resort affecting JV opportunities in Al Marjan?

Wynn Al Marjan — the first Wynn outside the US and the region's first integrated resort — has accelerated demand for hospitality-zoned plots nearby. JV opportunities in hotel development, serviced residences, and mixed-use projects around the resort are attracting significant international capital.

Reference: Arabian Business — Wynn RAK Coverage

Can a JV investment qualify me for a UAE Golden Visa?

Yes, if your share of the investment meets the AED 2 million threshold in UAE real estate and is structured in your name. Our specialists walk through visa eligibility during onboarding to ensure your JV structure meets the requirements.

Reference: UAE Government — Golden Visa
