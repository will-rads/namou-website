What landowners ask us.

How long does it typically take to sell a plot in RAK?

Timing varies by pricing, location, and readiness. With an exclusive mandate and proper positioning, serious plots in desirable zones (Al Marjan, Al Hamra, RAK Central) often transact within 3–6 months. Documentation readiness and realistic pricing are the two biggest factors.

Reference: Property Finder — RAK Market Trends

What taxes apply when I sell land in RAK?

The UAE levies no capital gains tax on property sales for individuals. Corporate tax of 9% may apply to business entities with profits above AED 375,000. Transfer fees and agency commissions apply at the point of sale and are typically split per the sale agreement.

Reference: PwC — UAE Tax Summary

What fees are involved in a RAK property transaction?

Expect approximately 4–5% in total transaction costs: RAK Municipality transfer fee (typically 2%), real estate agency commission (2%), title deed issuance, and legal/POA costs. Fees are paid at transfer and are typically split per the sale agreement.

Reference: Bayut — Cost of Property Transactions in the UAE

Why is now a good time to sell RAK land?

RAK has entered a rare alignment of rising demand, anchor projects (Wynn Al Marjan), and international investor interest. Serious buyers — developers, family offices, and hotel operators — are actively sourcing plots, creating strong leverage for well-positioned sellers.

Reference: Knight Frank — UAE Market Outlook
