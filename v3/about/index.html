About Namou

A licensed RAK brokerage built on land specialism.

We founded Namou Properties to bring the discipline of investment-grade real estate to RAK land transactions. We don't list apartments, we don't broker resale, we don't operate across emirates. We do one thing — RAK land — and we do it well.

Every plot we represent has been walked. Every number we present is modelled live, against transactions that closed in the last twelve months. The 20-minute video call is our default — it replaces three site visits and a week of email.
