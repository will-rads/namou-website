# Jad's Website Redesign Request

**Source:** Email from Jad Barghout (founder/head of Namou Properties)

## Key Points

1. **The website must become actionable** — not plain and simple. It needs to drive user action.
2. **Two main actions:** Buy or Sell Land and Commercial Properties.
3. **Highlight advantages of buying via Namou** — why buyers should choose Namou.
4. **Highlight advantages of selling via Namou** — why sellers should list with Namou.
5. **English and Arabic versions** — Arabic is a future consideration, not Phase 1.
6. **Broker collaborations section** — dedicated area for partner brokers.
7. **Design inspiration:** [starlink.com](https://starlink.com) — simple, clean, bold, modern.
